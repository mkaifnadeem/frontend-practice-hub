Spaghetti Aglio e Olio

Spaghetti Aglio e Olio is a classic Italian pasta dish known for its simplicity and bold flavors. It features spaghetti tossed with sautéed garlic in olive oil and a hint of red pepper flakes, creating a fragrant and slightly spicy sauce. The dish is quick to prepare, making it a perfect weeknight meal. Garnished with fresh parsley and optional Parmesan, it’s both comforting and elegant, showcasing the beauty of minimal ingredients

Here’s the item you needed

  • You'll need 400g of spaghetti
  • 6 cloves of garlic (sliced)
  • 1/2 cup of extra virgin olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon of red pepper flakes
  • Salt to taste

How to make it:

  1. Start by boiling a large pot of salted water and cooking the spaghetti until al dente
  2. Meanwhile, heat the olive oil in a large skillet over medium heat and add the sliced garlic
  3. Cooking until it turns golden but not burnt. Stir in the red pepper flakes and sauté for a minute
  4. Once the spaghetti is cooked, drain it, reserving some pasta water
  5. Add it to the skillet. Toss everything together
  6. Adding a splash of reserved pasta water if needed
  7. Season with salt and serve hot, garnished with fresh parsley and grated Parmesan cheese if desired.
